About This Reading Plan

Welcome to the Chronological Bible Reading Plan, a 364-day journey through Scripture arranged in historical order. This plan will guide you through the entire Bible, helping you understand the flow of God's redemptive story from Creation to the New Jerusalem.

How to Use This Plan

  • 52 Weeks: The plan is organized into 52 weekly sections, with each week containing 7 days of readings.
  • Daily Readings: Each day includes scripture references and key passages to guide your study.
  • Chronological Order: Unlike traditional reading plans, this follows the historical timeline of events.
  • Flexibility: Feel free to adjust the pace to fit your schedule. The goal is consistency, not perfection.

Tips for Success

  • Set aside a consistent time each day for reading
  • Keep a journal to record insights and reflections
  • Don't be discouraged if you miss a day—simply continue where you left off
  • Consider reading with a friend or small group for accountability
  • Pray before reading, asking God to reveal His truth to you
